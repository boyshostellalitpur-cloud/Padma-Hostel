Welcome to Padma Hostel

For nearly 10 years, Padma Hostel has set the standard for student and professional accommodation in Lalitpur. With a decade of experience in hospitality, we have built more than just a residence; we've built a community that fosters academic success, personal growth, and a true "home away from home" experience.

Located in the peaceful neighborhoods of Lalitpur, we offer comfortable, secure, and well-managed accommodation for students. With separate dedicated hostels for boys and girls, and multiple branches across Pulchowk and Kumaripati, we focus on creating a place where you can study, rest, and grow with confidence.
